Welcome to NGO

Non-governmental organizations (NGOs) are private, non-profit organizations that operate independently of government control. They are typically driven by a specific mission, such as humanitarian aid, environmental protection, or social justice. NGOs can operate at local, national, or international levels and often work in collaboration with governments, businesses, and other organizations to address social issues and advocate for change

They rely on donations, grants, and volunteer support to carry out their work. NGOs play a crucial role in filling gaps in government services, providing assistance to marginalized communities, and influencing policy decisions on a wide range of issues.
